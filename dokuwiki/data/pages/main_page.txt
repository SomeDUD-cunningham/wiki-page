====== Our Mission ======
The objective of this website is to hold leaders responsible by serving as a platform for coverage of global issues and topics that is less accessible for a general public. In our eyes, accessibility to information is a foundation of society. As such, we created InfoBay for the purpose of creating a centralised source of information for the exposure of information and corruption.

In addition, our team would like to thank the [[https://www.dokuwiki.org/dokuwiki|DokuWiki]] team for providing the software used to make this website, as it would not have been possible without them.
